Features

Everything you need
to get hired faster

Resuvia brings powerful AI analysis and practical career tools together in one mobile app.

🎯

ATS Score Analysis

Get a precise ATS compatibility score — not just a number. Resuvia breaks your resume down section by section, flags missing keywords, identifies weak language, and shows you exactly what a recruiter's screening software will penalize before any human sees your application.

✍️

AI-Powered Rewrites

Get more than suggestions — get actual rewrites. Resuvia generates ready-to-use alternative bullet points and summary lines, crafted for the specific job you are applying to. Replace weak language with AI-written copy that speaks the recruiter's language.

🔗

Analyze Any Job Posting Link

Paste a URL from any job board and Resuvia fetches the full description automatically — no copying walls of text. Works across most major job sites, including ones with heavy bot protection. Point, tap, analyze.

📋

Resume & Cover Letter Templates

Choose from 16 professionally designed templates — 6 free, 10 Pro — spanning styles from clean modern to executive. Export as a polished PDF or DOCX, ready to send without touching a desktop app.

🔍

Real Job Matches

Browse live listings from real job boards — Remotive, TheMuse, Arbeitnow, and more — filtered to your target role. No sponsored filler or ghost listings. One tap opens the original posting so you can apply directly.

Pro
🗺️

AI Career Roadmap & Interview Prep

Get a personalized plan built from your actual resume and target role — skill gaps to close, milestones to reach, and 10 tailored interview questions generated for that specific position. Not a generic template. A roadmap for your exact situation.

Pro
📂

Resume Version History

Every tailored version of your resume is saved — up to 20 — each encrypted on your device. Compare versions side by side with a built-in diff view, restore any previous copy, and never lose the version that landed you an interview.

Pro
✂️

Resume Refinement

Once you have your analysis, go further. The Refinement tool lets you iterate on specific sections — tightening language, adjusting tone, or targeting a related role — without running a full new analysis each time.

What sets Resuvia apart

Not just another
AI resume chatbot.

Generic AI tools give you a chatbot wrapper. Resuvia is purpose-built — private by design, reliable under load, and built for the phone in your pocket.

vs. upload-and-hope tools
🔒

Your resume never leaves your phone

Every other AI resume tool sends your document to their servers. Resuvia analyzes your PDF locally, then encrypts it with AES-256 and stores it on your device — not ours. Your job search stays between you and the employer.

vs. copy-paste tools
🔗

Paste a link, not a wall of text

Most tools make you copy and paste the entire job description yourself. Resuvia fetches it for you from any job posting URL — including the sites that try hardest to block automated access. Point, tap, done.

vs. single-model AI tools
⚡

A chain of AI models, not a single point of failure

Generic tools route everything through one AI. If it is overloaded, you wait. Resuvia cascades through a chain of frontier models — Gemini, Mistral, Claude — so if one is unavailable, the next takes over instantly. You always get a result.

vs. desktop-first tools
📱

Full power from your pocket

Other resume AI tools are browser tabs that fall apart on mobile. Resuvia is built for your phone — complete analysis, AI rewrites, real job listings, and career coaching, wherever you are. Job searching happens everywhere. Your tools should too.

🔒

Your resume stays on your device.

Unlike other tools, Resuvia never uploads your resume content to the cloud. Your document is analyzed and stored only on your phone — kept private and secure at all times. We do not sell your data, and we never will.
