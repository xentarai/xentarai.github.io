Account Management

Delete Your Resuvia Account

You can permanently delete your Resuvia account and all associated data at any time.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your Resuvia account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account record and profile (name, email address)
  • All resume metadata and file references
  • Encrypted resume cache stored on your device
  • Your resume version vault (Pro)
  • Your full analysis history and results
  • Application status tracking entries
  • Analytics and crash-reporting identifiers — anonymized and disassociated from your personal data
  • Active subscription tokens and purchase records associated with your account

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 — In-App (Recommended)

The fastest and most complete method. Your account is deleted immediately.

  1. Open the Resuvia app on your device.
  2. Tap Profile in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap Account preferences.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm your identity via a one-time verification code sent to your email.
  6. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — By Email

If you are unable to access the app, you may request deletion by email.

  1. Send an email to contactus@xentarai.com from the email address associated with your Resuvia account.
  2. Use the subject line: Resuvia Account Deletion Request.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation once complete.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • All personal data and resume content is removed immediately upon in-app deletion, or within 30 days for email requests.
  • Encrypted backups are purged within 30 days of the deletion request.
  • A minimal, fully anonymised billing record (no personal information) may be retained for up to 7 years as required by applicable tax and financial regulations. This record cannot be used to identify you.

Note on Local Data

Deleting your account via the app also wipes all locally cached data (encrypted resumes, vault entries, session tokens) from your device. If you delete by email, you should also manually uninstall the Resuvia app to remove local storage.
