Our Story

About Xentar AI

We're on a mission to make AI work for real people — through beautiful, intelligent mobile apps.

Who We Are

Xentar AI Inc. is a cutting-edge SaaS company building the next generation of AI-powered productivity applications for iOS and Android. We believe that the most powerful computer in the world is already in your pocket — and we're here to unlock its full potential.

Our Mission

We exist to transform the way people work. Not with complexity, not with bloat — but with intelligent, beautifully designed apps that understand your goals and get out of your way. Every product we ship starts with a single question: "What would this feel like if it just worked?"

Our Vision

A world where AI is a genuine productivity partner — not a gimmick. Where your phone helps you accomplish more meaningful work in less time, with less stress. Where the friction between your intentions and your outcomes simply disappears.

Why Mobile-First?

The smartphone has already replaced the laptop for billions of people around the world. Yet most productivity software is still designed for a desktop from 2010. We build for the way people actually work: on the go, in short bursts, across iOS and Android, with touch-first interfaces that feel native and effortless.

Our Values

  • AI-Native: Every feature is built around AI from day one — not added as an afterthought.
  • Privacy-First: Your data is yours. We design with security and privacy at the foundation.
  • Craft: We care deeply about the details — the animations, the copy, the edge cases.
  • Honesty: We ship real products that do real things. No vaporware, no hype.
